Discussions on planning ahead to integrate the AI Act concepts. Current status of the regulation is that it is in trilogue state (being discussed) and is expected to be published in early 2024. Similar to DGA, we can undertake identifying relevant concepts (e.g. entities, data categories), and mapping them to relevant concepts in core DPV or its extensions.
