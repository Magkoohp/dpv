DPIA Concepts

Discussing proposal for DPIA concepts expressed here - https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-dpvcg/2022May/0003.html

Most concepts have agreement amongst the group. The question of which concepts to add to main DPV and which ones to include externally, e.g. in an extension, was discussed with the conclusion that concepts also useful elsewhere, e.g. audits, would be in main DPV.

paul has highlighted the need for indicating "conditional acceptance" within an audit, such as where criteria are defined that must be carried out or met in order for the acceptance to be complete. There may or may not be further audits.

This concept is to be added as AuditConditionallyAccepted alongside other AuditStatus concepts.

To indicate the DPIA has been completed, or is ongoing, is done using ActivityStatus which enables expressing proposed, ongoing, halted, and completed.

For the DPIA specific concepts, such as its various states and outcomes will be added as part of DPV-GDPR since a DPIA is based on GDPR analysis and requirements. Alongside the concepts, there will be documentation for how to express DPIAs using DPV.

The Risk and Rights related concepts will be separated out into extensions as discussed earlier.
