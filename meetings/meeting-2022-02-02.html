Technology

georg: In the previous meeting, we discussed Technology as a concept, and agreed on its necessity and inclusion.
… Proposals related to specific forms of technology, e.g. database or algorithm, are being proposed.

jan: In these, does algorithm refer to, e.g. anonymisation algorithm? If so, how to express this?

harsh: We have anonymisation and pseudo-anonymisation as measures. It would be preferable to express concepts related to those under that e.g. specific types of anonymisation techniques.

harsh: That being said, there is no restriction to also declare those as types of algorithms if doing so is necessary or needed, e.g. in an implementation.
… ---
… Conclusion of discussion → For now, we include Technology as a concept, and for the rest, we look towards sources from ENISA ISO NIST / etc. for categories and concepts.
… We have the option to provide these as a separate extension if the concepts we collect and provide are opinionated rather than formal/universal.

Entities and their Categorisation

harsh: Currently in DPV, we have the DataController and similar concepts sitting alongside proposed concepts of NonProfitOrganisation and NonGovernmentalOrganisation.
… Proposal is to have concept LegalRole to provide a separation between concepts such as controller and processors from organisation types.

jan: IndustryConsortium as a broad categorisation of Organisation is okay as specific types of consortium could be extended if needed.
… The source of concepts is the ADMS vocabulary, with additional concepts added to balance expression e.g. Profit and NonProfit.

julian: In GDPR Art.91, there is "church and religious associations or communities" mentioned as an organisation. These should also be provided for completion.
… Discussion on what term to use for this did not conclude with any agreement. For now, we consider ReligiousAssociation as a proposed term awaiting further discussion.
… --
… ---
… The following concepts are accepted in today's meeting: LegalRole, JointDataControllers, Organisation, IndustryConsortium, GovernmentalOrganisation, NonGovernmentalOrganisation, ForProfitOrganisation, NonProfitOrganisation, AcademicScientificOrganisation, NationalAuthority, RegionalAuthority, SupraNationalAuthority
… Property hasEntity as parent property of entity-related properties.
… Term ThirdCountry to represent countries outside applicable jurisdiction (within whatever context it is used in).
