Scoring as Decision

See #87

<ghurlbot> Issue 87 Update concepts for Scoring and Decision to cover overlap (by coolharsh55) [concepts] [todo]

The group agrees that these concepts would be useful (for GDPR), but require careful consideration of implications as not every Scoring is a Decision, and moreso an Automated Decision Making.

Current modelling considers Scoring used in Decision to be a consideration for Automated Decision Making. Aim is to model these concepts (Scoring, Decision Making, Automated) and wait for CJEU decision before adding the implication to DPV-GDPR.

Note #87 this was discussed in today's meeting with the conclusion that concepts to represent Scoring and Decision Making should be present in DPV, but to wait for the CJEU decision before adding the implication of Automation to DPV-GDPR

Alignment with EUVOC

The decision to continue with DPV's modelling of location and jurisdictional concepts has been accepted in the interest of continued progress. If in the future we identify a better vocabulary or how to link with EUVOC, the existing work will incorporate these.

For consideration, the proposed remodelling also affects this work by separating the locations and jurisdiction specific concepts into different extensions. Locations would be in one extension, and the legal concepts would be in their respective jurisdiction specific extension, e.g. GDPR authorities would be in GDPR, Irish authority would be in Irish extension and so on.
