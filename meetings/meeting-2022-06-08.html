Resolution of Technology Concepts

The group agrees with the usefulness of these concepts.

There were concerns about the potential for this taxonomy to grow to a large size or to encapsulate various terms used for the same concepts. The rationale of the proposal is to provide a separate extension for this reason, and to choose one set of concepts for providing structure.

Julian had specific concerns about the expression of "Vulnerability" and "Vulnerability Exploitation" concepts because of the differences with how these terms are used (elsewhere).

These terms have now been merged under the common Security concept with variations for Detection, Prevention, Mitigation, and Monitoring.

The terms DigitalService and OnlineService have been removed as they do not relate to technologies and are covered by TechnologyProvisionMethod concept.

The terms TrackingCookie and TrackingPixel have been removed as they express the purpose (tracking) alongwith technology (cookie). Instead, the group agrees to provide only technologies within this technology and to have purposes specified through (main) DPV.

The term PIMS has been expanded to PersonalInformationManagementSystem to avoid ambiguity and confusion. The term PersonalDataStore has been removed as it expresses the same concept as and is a synonym for PIMS.

These changes will be reflected in the earlier proposal - https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-dpvcg/2022May/0007.html
