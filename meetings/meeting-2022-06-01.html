Proposed technology concepts

List of structured concepts shared on mailing list - https://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-dpvcg/2022May/0007.html

We will wait for comments/feedback and decide on these next week.

Mark says that ID and Surveillance should be the same concept, harsh disagrees and asks for clarification/justification to be shared on mailing list in reply to the proposal.

nishad asks about modelling authentication and whether this would be under ID or security, harsh says this should be under security (mirroring structure of tech/org measures)

The proposed concepts are not complete, but provide a top-concept or structure for the taxonomy. Authentication, authorisation, specific identification mechanisms and so on will be modelled under (subclasses) of these concepts, and combined where necessary.
