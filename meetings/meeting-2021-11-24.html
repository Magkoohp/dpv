Entity and Data Subject subclasses

Concept - Adult

Adult as subclass of DataSubject and inverse of Minor/Child ; for use to clarify 1) someone is not a child OR 2) someone is an adult (e.g. adult-only services)

julian: Does the model need the concept of NatualPerson as parent concept of Adult and Child to denote cases where a person is not a data subject
… discussion on creating a separate extension detailing relevant concepts for child-parent relationships and family, carers, guardians etc along with age of consent, age of adulthood in different regions/countries for convenience and use with DPV
… Concept - Authority subclasses for national, regional, and supranational
… no objections
… Concept - Organisation classification based on (non-)governmental and (not-)for-profit
… no objections , agreement on benefits of modelling these concepts
… For ConsumerOrganisation (e.g. consumer protection organisation or citizen rights organisations), it would be represented as part of NGO, no need of a separate concept
… Concept - AcademicScientificOrganisation
… Concept comes from ADMS vocabulary (the specific phrasing). Examples are universities, schools, etc.
… no objections , agreement on benefits
… Concept - Patient, Employee, Student
… no objections , agreement on need to represent these given existence of laws and measures
… Concept - Citizen, Non-Citizen, Immigrant, Tourist
… Discussion and debate with no conclusions based on several factors: how to represent citizenship and citizen rights (e.g.), residence vs immigrant, non-citizen and other concepts, tourist and relation to citizen
… For now we keep concepts as proposed and move on to next concepts
