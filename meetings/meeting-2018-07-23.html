workshop recap

AxelPollleres: I was also not there in person

<Bert> Report of the workshop on Data Privacy Controls and Vocabularies

Bert: workshop organized by SPECIAL project and w3c in April
… what technologies exist for privacy
… see what needs to be standardized and what not
… determine priorities for next steps
… first session was about things like how to express trust/access rights; identifiers; ODRL
… another session was about use cases in industry
… last session was about academic research
… modelling time, what about policies that change over time
… scientific datasets
… risks of collecting/combining/linking data
… we came up with 8 particular tasks
… ranked according to priority

<AxelPollleres> GDPR terms...should also be part of a vocabulary,

Bert: number 1 point was defining a privacy vocabulary

<AxelPollleres> taxonomy of purposes, taxonomy of perosnal data, log data, linkage

<AxelPollleres> human behaviour.

Bert: Taxonomy of regulatory privacy terms (including all GDPR terms). Taxonomy for personal data. Taxonomy of purposes. Taxonomy of disclosure. Metadata related to the details of anonymisation. Log vocabulary. Taxonomy of linkage operations. Taxonomies of human behavior.
